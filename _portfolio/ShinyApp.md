These screenshots are a preview of a R Shiny app that I have been building to help experimenters quickly visualise and analyse platelet aggregometry data. I have developed two versions of this app, one designed to be deployed online on the Shiny Live platform without figure download capabilities due to compatibility issues, and another full-featured version for users to download and run locally. This app is part of a [platelet aggregometry data analysis pipeline](https://www.jove.com/t/62947/kinetx-combined-flow-cytometry-assay-analysis-software-framework-to) developed at the University of Reading by Dr. Joanne Dunster and was developed under the supervision of Dr Dunster and Prof. J. M. Gibbins.
